Haryana Widow Pension Scheme

Through Haryana Vidhwa Pension Yojana , financial assistance will be provided to those women of Haryana whose husband has died so that they can live their lives. This financial assistance will be of ₹ 2250 per month. To get the benefit of this scheme, the annual income of the applicant woman's family should be ₹ 200000 or less. If the applicant woman is getting the benefit of any other pension scheme of the government, then she cannot take advantage of this scheme. To get the benefit of this scheme, the applicant woman is required to submit the husband's death certificate and it is mandatory for the woman to have a bank account in a nationalized bank.

Apart from this, the age of the applicant woman should be 18 years or more, only then she will be able to get the benefit of this scheme. Through Haryana Widow Pension Scheme, women of the state will become self-reliant and empowered and their economic condition will also improve.
