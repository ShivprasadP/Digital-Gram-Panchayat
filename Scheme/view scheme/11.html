Viklang Pension Yojana 2022

Disabled Pension Scheme has been launched by the Central Government . Through this scheme, pension is provided to the disabled citizens of the country every month. Under this scheme, their own contribution is made by the Central and State Governments. The central government contributes ₹ 200 per person per month and the state government provides the remaining amount. The minimum rate of providing pension under this scheme is ₹ 400 per month. The maximum state pension of ₹ 500 per month is provided to the beneficiaries. This amount varies from state to state. The pension amount is disbursed directly to the beneficiary's account through Direct Benefit Transfer. Through this scheme, the disabled citizens of the country will become strong and self-reliant.

Objective of Disabled Pension Scheme

The main objective of Viklang Pension Yojana 2022 s to provide pension to the disabled citizens of the country . So that he can become strong and self-reliant. Now the disabled citizens of the country will not need to depend on anyone for their expenses. Because they will be provided pension by the state government. This pension will be distributed in the account of the beneficiaries on monthly or quarterly or half yearly basis. Beneficiaries do not even need to go to any government office to apply under this scheme. They can apply under this scheme through the official website sitting at home. This will save both time and money and bring transparency in the system.

Eligibility for Disability Pension Scheme

  • The applicant should be a permanent resident of the state from where he/she has applied.
  • The minimum age of the applicant should be 18 years.
  • The maximum age of the candidate should be 59 years.
  • Applicant should have minimum 40% disability.
  • If the applicant is being provided the benefit of any other pension scheme then he is not eligible to get the benefit of this scheme.
  • Applicant should be below poverty line.
