The procedure and related information to apply for benefits under Chhatrapati Shivaj

  • The link of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Kisan Samman Yojana 2022 – Chatrapati Shivaji Shetkari Sanmaan Yojana 2022 / CSMSSY 2022 has been made available at https://aaplesarkar.maharashtra.gov.in/en/ .
  • After clicking on the link regarding Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Kisan Samman Yojana 2022, new tab for application will open on http://www.csmssy.in portal.
  • The definite method of registering the applicant, the decision of the government and other details of the scheme will be available on this portal.

Chatrapati Shivaji Shetkari Sanmaan Yojana

  • If the applicant does not have Aadhar Number / UID Number for each member of his/her family and his/her family (family i.e. applicant, spouse and member of working age below 18 years), then applicants need to apply for EID based or new Aadhaar Number. Must register first
  • On this page, you have to click on “New Registration – New Registration” and will be asked whether the applicant has Aadhaar number. If you select “Yes”, then two options “OTP – OTP” and “Biometric – Biometrics”are available on the website.
  • For “OTP – OTP” based authentication, One Time Password / One Time Password will be sent to the registered mobile number (which is linked with Aadhaar number). In case the mobile number is not linked with the Aadhaar number, he/she will have to first link the Aadhaar number with the mobile number by visiting the nearest help center.
  • In case the applicant or family member does not have an Aadhaar number, he/she can register it through the option of unbanked registration on the web portal. However, after the applicant receives the Aadhar number, he has to update it on the web portal to take advantage of the scheme.
  • On successful registration of one of the above methods, the applicant and his/her family members can choose the individual User ID and correct password as per availability.
  • Now the applicant has to login to the portal http://www.csmssy.in with his User Id and Password.
  • If the applicant selects the “Loan Waiver” link available in the application then the loan application form will appear on the computer.

Chatrapati Shivaji Shetkari Sanmaan Yojana

  • If the applicant has registered through Aadhar number, then the basic information about the applicant like name, support number, linking mobile number, date of birth, address etc. will already be shown in the loan waiver application. All the information related to Aadhaar number will be available automatically.
  • After that the applicant will now have to fill PAN card, pension PPO details, loan details etc. in the application form. In the application, each family (applicant, spouse and under-18) will have the option to fill information about more than one loan.
  • The applicant will also have to fill in the information of his/her spouse and the under-18 borrower on the online portal, based on the information provided in his/her application.
  • After submitting the application online, the applicant will receive the acknowledgment receipt by the computer system. The applicant can take a copy of this receipt or download the PDF file for his future use.
  • It is suggested that the applicant should keep his/her User Id and Password safe as it will be useful for you in future also
